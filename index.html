My current research focuses on enforcing perceptual consistency in algorithms that evaluate error/quality of images and applications of such a metric. A recent publication at CVPR2018 provides more details. I am also interested in related problems such as attention modeling, gaze prediction, and also, in 3D computer vision.

My time in grad school is split between my own research and providing technical mentorship (which I thoroughly enjoy!) to seniors for their capstone projects on computer vision and machine learning. I also enjoy running long distances, painting and taking pictures.
